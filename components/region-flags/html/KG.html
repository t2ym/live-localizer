The 40 rays or lines are the 40 tribes of Kyrgyzstan who united to fight Chingiz Khan or Genghis Khan (Temujin), the Mongol Leader who conquered most of Asia in 1200. The center figure is the view from inside the yurta looking up at the rising sun.
